Personalized Timetable for Autistic Learners

Our specialized timetable model is designed specifically for children on the autism spectrum, focusing on structured routines, sensory integration, and individualized pacing.

Each schedule is customized based on the child's unique needs, strengths, and sensory preferences, incorporating visual supports and predictable transitions.

The timetable balances learning activities with necessary breaks, sensory regulation periods, and social skills development in a controlled, supportive environment.
